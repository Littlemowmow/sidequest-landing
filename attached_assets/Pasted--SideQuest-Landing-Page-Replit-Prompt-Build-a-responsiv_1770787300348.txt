## WHAT SIDEQUEST IS

SideQuest is an iOS travel app so the trip doesn't die in the groupchat. Instead of exciting travel ideas fizzling out in endless message threads, SideQuest surfaces hidden gems, local secrets, and authentic experiences ‚Äî then lets groups actually plan and go together.

**Tagline:** "so the trip doesn't die in the groupchat"

**Target users:** Study abroad students (initial wedge), expanding to broader travel market.

**The problem we solve:** You and your friends are hyped about a trip. Someone texts "we should totally go to Barcelona." Everyone's in. Then... 47 unread messages later, nobody's booked anything, nobody knows what to do there, and the trip dies in the groupchat. SideQuest fixes that.

**Three core features:**
1. **Discover** ‚Äî Swipe through curated activities like Hinge, but for travel. Each card shows a hidden gem, local pick, or must-do experience with beautiful photography.
2. **Group Trips** ‚Äî Create a trip, invite friends with a code, propose activities, and vote on what to do. Democracy, not whoever-texts-loudest.
3. **Curated Content** ‚Äî Every activity is categorized:
   - üü¢ **SideQuest** ‚Äî Hidden gems worth discovering (secret rooftop bars, underground jazz clubs, locals-only markets)
   - üîµ **Local Pick** ‚Äî What residents actually do (the ramen shop with no English menu, the park where everyone watches sunset)
   - üü° **Tourist Essential** ‚Äî Worthwhile mainstream stuff that's actually worth your time (not tourist traps)

## DESIGN RULES

1. **Typography:** Use Inter, DM Sans, or similar clean sans-serif from Google Fonts. Headlines bold, body regular. Let the text breathe.

2. **Spacing:** Generous. Every section has lots of padding (80-120px vertical between sections). Cards have 24-32px internal padding. Nothing should feel cramped.

3. **Cards:** White (#FFFFFF) with subtle box-shadow (`0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`), border-radius 16-20px. This is the core visual pattern from the Tino concept.

4. **Buttons:** 
   - Primary: `#2D5A3D` background, white text, border-radius 50px (full pill), padding 16px 32px, font-weight 600
   - Secondary: transparent, `#2D5A3D` border, `#2D5A3D` text
   - Hover: darken slightly, subtle scale transform

5. **Images:** Use high-quality Unsplash travel photos. Search terms: "friends traveling europe", "barcelona street", "tokyo temple", "rome sunset", "group travel adventure". Make them feel authentic, not stocky.

6. **Animations:** Subtle scroll-triggered fade-in for sections. Nothing flashy. The Tino concept is calm and intentional.

7. **Mobile responsive:** Must look great on phone since our users are mobile-first study abroad students.

8. **No dark mode needed** for the landing page. Light sage is the vibe.

9. **Badges/pills:** Small rounded pills with category colors (#6B9B74 green for SideQuest, #5B9BD5 blue for Local Pick, #C4A265 gold for Tourist Essential) on light tinted backgrounds.

10. **The iMessage mockup** in the problem section should look like actual iOS messages ‚Äî gray bubbles on left (incoming), blue on right (sent), with the rounded shape and spacing of real iMessages. This is the emotional hook of the page.
